PHASE 1 — Ingestion
   RSS feeds + scrapers
   → raw article store (title, body, outlet, timestamp)

PHASE 2 — Feature Extraction (deterministic)
   • Lexicon-based signals
     - sentiment lexicons (VADER)
     - political word lists (loaded terms, dog whistles)
     - emotion lexicons (anger, fear, hope)
   • Structural signals
     - who gets quoted (named entity recognition)
     - what's in headline vs. body (framing analysis)
     - passive vs. active voice ratio
   • Topic modeling (BERTopic or LDA)
     → consistent, reproducible, no LLM involved

PHASE 3 — Classification (BERT layer)
   • Fine-tuned BERT for political leaning
   • Fine-tuned BERT for tension/conflict scoring
   • Trained on YOUR labeled dataset (see below)
   → deterministic scores, versioned models

PHASE 4 — LLM as labeler (offline, one-time)
   • Used ONLY to generate training labels
   • Run same article through LLM N times,
     take majority vote → reduces variance
   • Human spot-check a sample
   → this is where LLM lives, not in production

PHASE 5 — Aggregation
   • Daily outlet-level political position
   • Topic polarity (variance across outlets)
   • Tension index (composite of conflict signals)

PHASE 6 — Card generation
   → Instagram output
